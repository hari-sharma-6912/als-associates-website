About Us

A.L.S Associates is a specialized provider of high-precision RF and microwave components used in satellite communication, aerospace systems, and advanced research projects. Our products include high-performance amplifiers, microwave power dividers, and electronic components designed for demanding environments in space technology. The company contributes components and engineering solutions for research organizations, aerospace laboratories, and satellite communication programs across the world.
